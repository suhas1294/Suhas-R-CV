Academic Project :

Year

: 2015

Semister

: 8th

Title

: “Adaptation of internationally standardized number system for visually challenged ”

Problem :


  1. The visually challenged children currently use Braille system for recognizing numbers and doing mathematical operations.
  2. Currently Visually challenged children are not able to continue their higher education with their own effort of reproducing whatever they have learnt [limited till class 10 in state of Karnataka].
  3. For a simple operation of two digit addition, they take about 10 minutes to solve.
  4. They consume much time in recognizing number, punching the (braille) number into a paper (to make perforations as in braille).

Solution :


  1. Our project is an effort to adapt internationally accepted number system (in place of Braille) for the visually challenged.
  2. Our project model consisted of both Hardware and Firmware. The Hardware was built upon PIC Microcontroller, Pop-up system and servo motors. The Firmware written using C-Programming.
  3. Upon working of the model, visually challenged students were successfully able to recognize the digits which helped them to learn mathematics easily.

Output :


  1. The visually challenged students were able to recognize international used numbers with ease.
  2. Upon recognition, they could perform basic mathematical operations faster than before.
  3. In future instead of conventional way like Braille number system, they could switch over to international number system.
